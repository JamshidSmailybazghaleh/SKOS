# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

MASTER-INDEX.md is the official entry point and navigation document of the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This document provides a unified map of the entire SKOS ecosystem, including:

- Architecture
- Documentation
- Builds
- Engines
- Mission Control
- Knowledge Structures
- Roadmap
- Releases

The purpose of this document is to provide a single reference point for understanding, navigating, and managing SKOS.

---

# 1.2 Vision

SKOS is designed as a Knowledge Operating System.

The vision of SKOS is to create an organized, structured, and intelligent memory system for human knowledge and experience.

SKOS connects:

- Knowledge
- Information
- Documents
- Systems
- Artificial Intelligence
- Human Experience

into an integrated ecosystem.

---

# 1.3 Mission

The mission of SKOS is:

> To design and develop a scalable operating system for managing, organizing, preserving, and expanding human knowledge.

---

# 1.4 Core Philosophy

SKOS is built upon the following principles:

## Knowledge Organization

Information must be structured before it becomes usable knowledge.

---

## Single Source Of Truth

Every piece of information must have one authoritative location.

---

## Traceability

Every change must be recorded and identifiable.

---

## Continuous Evolution

The system must continuously learn, improve, and expand.

---

## Human-AI Collaboration

SKOS is designed to connect human intelligence with artificial intelligence.
