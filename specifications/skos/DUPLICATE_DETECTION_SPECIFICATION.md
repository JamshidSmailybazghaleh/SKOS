# Duplicate Detection Specification

Version: 0.0.1

---

## Purpose

Detect duplicated or highly similar knowledge objects while preserving valuable information.

---

## Detection Levels

Level 1

Exact Duplicate

Same content

---

Level 2

Near Duplicate

Minor wording changes

---

Level 3

Revision

New edition of existing object

---

Level 4

Translation

Same knowledge in another language

---

Level 5

Derived Content

Partially extracted from another object

---

## Decision Policy

Exact Duplicate

Merge

---

Near Duplicate

Human Review

---

Revision

Link to Previous Version

---

Translation

Create Translation Relationship

---

Derived Content

Create Derived Relationship

---

## Rule

No duplicate is permanently deleted.

All duplicate decisions remain traceable.
