Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Ecosystem Management Platform Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to manage the complete SKOS ecosystem as an integrated operational environment.

---

Objective

Create a centralized ecosystem management platform capable of supervising repositories, engines, services, users, workspaces, publications, governance, analytics, and future distributed knowledge nodes.

---

Vision

SKOS shall operate as a unified knowledge ecosystem rather than a collection of independent software components.

Every subsystem shall be continuously monitored, coordinated, measured, and governed from a single ecosystem management platform.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Ecosystem Management Platform Foundation
- Ecosystem Registry
- Component Lifecycle Management
- Ecosystem Health Monitoring
- Resource Coordination
- Dynamic JSON Integration

Historical Significance

BUILD-000259 establishes the operational management layer for the entire SKOS ecosystem.

This Build enables centralized supervision, coordination, and optimization of every component within the Knowledge Operating System, preparing SKOS for large-scale deployment and future distributed knowledge networks.

It marks the transition from managing individual systems to managing a complete knowledge ecosystem.
