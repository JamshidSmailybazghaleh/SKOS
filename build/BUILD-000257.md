Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Collaboration Platform Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to support collaborative knowledge creation, review, discussion, governance, and shared decision-making.

---

Objective

Create a unified collaboration platform that enables individuals, teams, organizations, and intelligent services to work together within the SKOS ecosystem.

---

Vision

Knowledge shall become a collaborative asset.

Every contribution shall be traceable, reviewable, version-controlled, and attributable.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Collaboration Platform Foundation
- Collaboration Registry
- Workspace Management
- Team Coordination
- Review & Discussion Framework
- Dynamic JSON Integration
