Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Global Knowledge Archive Foundation.

This Build introduces the permanent preservation infrastructure required to store, protect, organize, and maintain long-term access to all SKOS knowledge assets.

---

Objective

Create a global knowledge archive framework capable of preserving publications, research outputs, historical records, versions, metadata, and intellectual heritage across generations.

---

Vision

SKOS shall become a permanent memory infrastructure for knowledge.

Every valuable knowledge artifact shall have:

- Preservation
- Identity
- Provenance
- Version History
- Long-Term Accessibility

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Global Knowledge Archive Framework
- Archive Registry
- Preservation Management
- Digital Preservation Policies
- Metadata Preservation
- Recovery Framework
- Dynamic JSON Integration
