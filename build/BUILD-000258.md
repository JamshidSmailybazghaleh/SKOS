Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Governance Platform Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the governance infrastructure required to define, enforce, monitor, and evolve policies governing the entire SKOS knowledge ecosystem.

---

Objective

Create a unified governance framework capable of managing policies, standards, responsibilities, approvals, compliance, and organizational accountability.

---

Vision

Every knowledge object, engine, service, workflow, and publication shall operate under transparent, auditable, and standardized governance policies.

Governance shall become a core architectural capability rather than an external administrative process.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Governance Platform Foundation
- Governance Registry
- Policy Management
- Compliance Framework
- Decision Registry
- Dynamic JSON Integration
