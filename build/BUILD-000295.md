Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Global Knowledge Civilization Intelligence Network Foundation.

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to connect human knowledge, cultural heritage, historical memory, collective intelligence, and global knowledge networks into an integrated civilization-scale intelligence framework.

Create a civilization intelligence network capable of representing, preserving, analyzing, and connecting humanity's accumulated knowledge and experiences across time, geography, and disciplines.

- Scientific Knowledge
- Cultural Heritage
- Historical Experience
- Philosophical Thought
- Social Knowledge
- Human Creativity
