Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Global Knowledge Security Intelligence Foundation.

This Build introduces the security infrastructure required to protect knowledge assets, maintain trust, control access, verify integrity, and ensure safe operation across the SKOS ecosystem.

---

Objective

Create an intelligent security framework capable of protecting distributed knowledge resources, autonomous agents, communication channels, and federation networks while maintaining transparency and governance.

SKOS shall become a trusted knowledge operating ecosystem.

Every knowledge asset shall have:

- Identity
- Ownership
- Integrity
- Access Control
- Trust Level
- Security History
