Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Digital Twin Framework Foundation.

This Build introduces a living digital representation of the SKOS ecosystem capable of monitoring system states, modeling architecture, tracking changes, and supporting simulation-driven decisions.

---

Objective

Create a Digital Twin framework that mirrors the operational, structural, and knowledge states of SKOS to enable monitoring, analysis, prediction, and controlled evolution.

---

Vision

SKOS shall maintain a living digital model of itself.

The Digital Twin shall provide a continuously updated representation of:

- Architecture
- Engines
- Knowledge Assets
- Processes
- Performance
- Evolution History

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Digital Twin Framework Foundation
- System State Modeling
- Architecture Mapping
- Live Synchronization
- State Monitoring
- Predictive Analysis
- Dynamic JSON Integration
