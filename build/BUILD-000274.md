Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Knowledge Optimization Engine Foundation.

This Build introduces the optimization infrastructure required to improve knowledge workflows, resource allocation, learning paths, research processes, and ecosystem performance.

---

Objective

Create an optimization engine capable of analyzing knowledge structures, evaluating alternatives, and selecting optimal solutions based on defined objectives, constraints, and intelligence models.

---

Vision

SKOS shall not only understand and predict knowledge processes but shall continuously improve them.

The system shall identify the most efficient paths for knowledge creation, discovery, learning, preservation, and utilization.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Optimization Engine Foundation
- Optimization Registry
- Objective Management
- Constraint Framework
- Optimization Models
- Performance Improvement Analysis
- Dynamic JSON Integration
