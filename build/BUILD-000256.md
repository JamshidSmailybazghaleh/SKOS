Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Intelligence Platform Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to transform knowledge, analytics, reasoning, and learning into actionable intelligence.

---

Objective

Create a unified intelligence platform capable of generating insights, recommendations, predictions, and decision-support information from the SKOS knowledge ecosystem.

---

Vision

SKOS shall evolve beyond knowledge management into an intelligent knowledge platform capable of assisting human decision-making through structured analysis and explainable intelligence.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Intelligence Platform Foundation
- Intelligence Registry
- Insight Generation
- Recommendation Framework
- Decision Support Services
- Dynamic JSON Integration
