Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Quality Assurance Engine Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to evaluate, monitor, and continuously improve the quality of all knowledge assets.

---

Objective

Create a unified quality assurance framework capable of measuring knowledge quality, consistency, completeness, and reliability.

---

Vision

Every knowledge object shall be continuously evaluated against defined quality standards before and after publication.

Knowledge quality shall become a measurable and manageable characteristic of the SKOS ecosystem.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Quality Assurance Engine Foundation
- Quality Registry
- Validation Framework
- Review Workflow
- Quality Metrics
- Dynamic JSON Integration
