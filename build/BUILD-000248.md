Purpose

Establish the first Knowledge Analytics Engine Foundation for the Smaily Knowledge Operating System (SKOS).

This Build introduces the infrastructure required to measure, analyze, and visualize the evolution and performance of the SKOS knowledge ecosystem.

---

Objective

Create a unified analytics framework capable of transforming operational and knowledge data into meaningful indicators, reports, and decision-support metrics.

---

Vision

SKOS shall continuously measure the growth, quality, usage, connectivity, and operational health of its knowledge ecosystem.

Analytics shall support strategic planning, operational monitoring, and continuous improvement.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Analytics Engine Foundation
- Analytics Registry
- Metrics Processing
- Statistical Dashboard
- Trend Analysis
- Dynamic JSON Integration
