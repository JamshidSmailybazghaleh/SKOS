Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Global Knowledge Intelligence Core Foundation.

This Build introduces the central intelligence layer responsible for integrating knowledge analysis, reasoning, learning, monitoring, and decision-support capabilities across the SKOS ecosystem.

Objective

Create a unified intelligence core capable of processing knowledge signals, coordinating intelligence services, generating insights, and supporting intelligent system operations.

---

Vision

SKOS shall operate with a unified intelligence core that connects all knowledge capabilities into one coordinated intelligence architecture.

The Intelligence Core shall become the cognitive center of SKOS.
