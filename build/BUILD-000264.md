Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Universal Knowledge Identity Framework Foundation.

This Build introduces the global identity infrastructure required to uniquely identify every knowledge object, entity, concept, relationship, repository, engine, service, and federation member within the SKOS ecosystem.

---

Objective

Create a globally unique, persistent, version-aware identity framework that guarantees long-term identification, traceability, interoperability, and citation of all knowledge assets.

---

Vision

Every knowledge asset within SKOS shall possess one permanent and globally unique identity.

That identity shall remain stable regardless of storage location, organizational ownership, system migration, language, or software version.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Universal Knowledge Identity Framework Foundation
- Global Identity Registry
- Persistent Identifier Management
- Identity Resolution Services
- Identity Validation
- Dynamic JSON Integration
