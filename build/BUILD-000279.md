Purpose

Establish the first SKOS Knowledge Governance Intelligence Framework Foundation.

This Build introduces the intelligent governance infrastructure required to manage policies, standards, compliance, ethics, permissions, and decision controls across the SKOS ecosystem.

---

Objective

Create a governance intelligence framework capable of monitoring, evaluating, and enforcing governance rules across distributed SKOS nodes, autonomous agents, knowledge processes, and federation members.

---

Vision

SKOS shall evolve as a trusted knowledge ecosystem where intelligence and autonomy operate within transparent governance boundaries.

Governance shall become an intelligent, adaptive, and continuously improving layer of the system.

---

Scope

This Build includes:

- Knowledge Governance Intelligence Foundation
- Governance Registry
- Policy Management
- Compliance Monitoring
- Ethics Framework
- Decision Governance
- Audit Intelligence
- Dynamic JSON Integration
