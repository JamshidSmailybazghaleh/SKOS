# SKOS

**Smaily Knowledge Operating System**

---

## Vision

Building a structured knowledge operating system to preserve, organize, connect and evolve human knowledge.

---

## Mission

SKOS is the foundational knowledge infrastructure of the Smaily Knowledge Foundation.

Its purpose is to transform information into structured knowledge and preserve valuable human experience for future generations.
